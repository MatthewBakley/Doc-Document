Moveset

Doc Moveset Frame Data
This guide will talk more in terms of application.

Jabs

Jab 1

Frame 2
One of Docs most useful neutral mixup tools, it can set up for things like downsmash and grab.
Great for interrupting opponents movement and beating longer startup moves.
Ends frame 15 making it a great option to call out floaty landings as well.

Jab 2

Frame 2
Not often utilized and for good reason, its basically just jab 1 but you have to do jab 1 to set it up, which means that it can be used as an additional mixup out of Jab 1, but not much else.

Jab 3

Frame 4
The same as jab 2 except has some knockback to it so it can be a get off me tool at times and it can work on shield some what well, also has one of the lowest reaches of any of his moves at ledge.

Dash Attack

Frame 6
Situational combo tool
Decent burst option
Sends opponents straight up
Can cause doc to “low profile” certain moves.
Leads to a techchase, especially on fast fallers.

Tilts

Down Tilt

Frame 5
Used as a mixup to confuse your opponent in scraps to lead to a tech chase or edgeguard.
Much more practically used to set up edgeguards at around mid-high%, however can be amsah teched.

Forward Tilt

Frame 4
Docs best poke tool, if its spaced well it can't be punished on whiff by some characters and can't be punished on shield.
It's also useful for edgeguarding on stage as well. The low angle can cover many options for grabbing ledge.
It can set up for a techchase situation too.

Up Tilt

Frame 4
A great combo tool and docs best anti air
The first frame hitbox sends opponent away and can set up for edgeguards at high %s.
Its hitbox lingers and it doesn't have much lag either.
Best used with dthrow, uthrow, landing uair, and dash attack.

Smash Attacks

Down Smash

Frame 5
Easily docs best smash attack, amazing kill move and counter move.
Can set up for techchases can be used in techchases.
Can cover rolls with back hit.
Can be hard to punish on shield if spaced well.
It really does just about everything a move can do. When in doubt, downsmash.

Forward Smash

Frame 12 Doc's best kill move, but poor range Extremely good reward off of :

  • Techchases
  • Hard Reads
  • Uair/DI mix up Finisher
  • Punishing Undershoots
  • Punishing Laggy Up-B Recoveries i.e. Shiek and Falcon

Up Smash

Frame 9 (Frame 10 OoS) The worst of the smash attacks but not bad by any means. It's mainly more useful against characters like yoshi who you can't hit anything else with out of dthrow, so floaties.
It also has some use when opponents are coming down due to hurtbox shift and head invincibilty like Falco laser (needs testing).
If the opponent is grounded it can setup for kills since it spikes them (grounded only)
Great for chain grab finishers

Aerial Attacks

Bair

Frame 6 (Frame 10 oos)
Doc's second best edgeguarding and poking tool extremely good for walling out and dealing with CC.
Can be used as an approach option and a retreat option
Due to its low amount of end lag, doc can sh bair and be actionable before they hit the ground.
Can waveland out of instant sh bair.

Dair

Frame 10 (Frame 14 oos)
Good for reading sh and fh.
Can combo into a landing aerial or a dj aerial.
can be good to come down with too, just don't overuse, especially not aggressively.
Auto cancels out of a full hop.
Combos into jab on autocancel.
